FIFA 24

FIFA is a series of soccer simulation video games developed and published by EA Sports. The game allows players to experience and participate in professional soccer matches with teams from various leagues around the world. Players can choose to play as a single player or as part of a team in various game modes, including a career mode, online multiplayer matches, and tournaments. In the game, players control individual players or the entire team and use various tactics and strategies to score goals and win matches. The game features realistic graphics, physics, and gameplay, as well as licensed teams and players from various leagues.

WWE 2K24

WWE is a professional wrestling video game series developed by Yuke's and published by 2K Sports. The game allows players to experience and participate in professional wrestling matches with wrestlers from the WWE roster. Players can choose to play as a single player or as part of a team in various game modes, including a career mode, online multiplayer matches, and tournaments. In the game, players control individual wrestlers and use various moves and abilities to defeat their opponents. The game features realistic graphics, physics, and gameplay, as well as licensed wrestlers, arenas, and other elements from the WWE.

GTA VI

Grand Theft Auto (GTA) series is a well-known action-adventure game series developed by Rockstar Games. The games are set in fictional cities and allow players to explore and interact with the environment and participate in various activities, such as completing missions, driving vehicles, and engaging in combat. The series is known for its open-world gameplay, high-speed chases, and various other action-packed elements..
